CARING TIPS
  • » Always provide clean, fresh water for your cat.

  • » Feed your cat nutritious, high-quality food.

  • » Clean the litter box daily to maintain hygiene.

  • » Schedule regular check-ups and vaccinations.

  • » Brush your cat’s coat regularly, especially long-haired breeds.

  • » Ensure your cat has a quiet, secure area for resting.

  • » Use toys and scratching posts to keep your cat active.

  • » Let your cat approach affection on their terms.

  • » Clean your cat’s teeth or offer dental treats.

  • » Keep your cat indoors to protect them from dangers.
